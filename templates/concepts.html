Key Powerlifting Concepts & Exercises

Improving your strength requires focusing on key principles and using the right exercises to target weak points in your lifts.


Progressive Overload

Gradually increasing weight or volume over time is essential for strength development.

  • Barbell Squat
  • Bench Press
  • Deadlift

Technique & Stability

Strong technique ensures efficient movement and reduces injury risk.

  • Paused Squats
  • Tempo Bench Press
  • Deficit Deadlifts

Lockout Strength

Many lifters struggle at the top of lifts. These exercises help improve lockout power.

  • Board Press
  • Rack Pulls
  • Hip Thrusts

Speed & Power

Explosiveness helps you move through sticking points more effectively.

  • Box Squats
  • Speed Bench Press
  • Kettlebell Swings

Core Strength

A strong core is essential for stability across all lifts.

  • Planks
  • Hanging Leg Raises
  • Ab Wheel Rollouts
